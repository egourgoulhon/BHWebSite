Introduction to black hole physics

Lectures at Gravitational Waves 2018

Les Houches, France, 2-27 July 2018

These pages accompany Éric Gourgoulhon 's lectures at Les Houches Summer School Gravitational Waves 2018.

The lectures introduce some aspects of black hole physics. The prerequisite is some basic knowledge of general relativity, at the level of an introductory course, as in Thomas Moore's lectures at the same summer school.

You will find here:

  • the slides and videos of the lectures
  • the lecture notes, as well as their sources (LaTeX and Inkscape files)
  • some SageMath notebooks for computations involved in the course

Éric Gourgoulhon / 2018-07-04
