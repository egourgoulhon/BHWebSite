Geometry and physics of black holes

Lectures at Dubna International Advanced School of Theoretical Physics

Dubna, 11-19 May 2017

These pages accompany the lectures delivered by Éric Gourgoulhon in the framework of the Dubna International Advanced School of Theoretical Physics.

The lectures introduce some aspects of black hole physics via a geometrical approach. The prerequisite is some basic knowledge of general relativity, at the level of an introductory course.

You will find here:

  • the plan of the courses
  • the slides of the figures illustrating the courses
  • the lecture notes, as well as their sources (LaTeX and Inkscape files)
  • some SageMath worksheets for computations involved in the course
