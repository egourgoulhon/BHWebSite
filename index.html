Geometry and physics of black holes

These pages are devoted to Éric Gourgoulhon's lectures at various places:

  • Advanced Lectures of Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris, Paris, France, March-April 2016 [course page]
  • Chaire Georges Lemaître, UCLouvain, Louvain-la-Neuve, Belgium, November 2016 [course page]
  • Dubna International Advanced School of Theoretical Physics, Dubna, Russia, May 2017 [course page]
  • Summer School Gravitational Waves 2018, Les Houches, France, July 2018 [course page]
  • School on Black Holes and Gravitational Waves, Chennai, India, January 2022 [course page]
  • PSL graduate programs in Astrophysics and in Physics, ENS, Paris, France, May-June 2023 [course page]

The lectures introduce some aspects of black hole physics. The prerequisite is some basic knowledge of general relativity, at the level of an introductory course.

You will find here:

  • the lecture notes, as well as their sources (LaTeX, Inkscape and SageMath files)
  • the slides and videos of the lectures
  • some SageMath notebooks for computations involved in the course
