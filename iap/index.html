Geometry and physics of black holes

IAP Advanced Lectures

Paris, March-April 2016

These pages accompany the lectures delivered by Éric Gourgoulhon in the framework of the Advanced Lectures of Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris.

The lectures have also been given in Louvain-la-Neuve (2016), Dubna (2017) and Les Houches (2018).

The lectures introduce some aspects of black hole physics via a geometrical approach. The prerequisite is to have some notions of general relativity, at the level of an introductory course.

You will find here:

  • the plan and videos of the courses
  • the lecture notes, as well as their sources (LaTeX and Inkscape files)
  • some SageMath notebooks for computations involved in the course
  • the mailing list
