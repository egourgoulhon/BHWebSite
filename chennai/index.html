Basics of black hole physics

Lectures at the School on Black Holes and Gravitational Waves

Centre for Strings, Gravitation and Cosmology

Chennai, India, 17-20 January 2022 (online)

These pages accompany Éric Gourgoulhon's online lectures at the School on Black Holes and Gravitational Waves at the Centre for Strings, Gravitation and Cosmology of the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (Chennai, India) on 17-20 January 2022.

The lectures introduce some aspects of black hole physics. The prerequisite is some basic knowledge of general relativity, at the level of an introductory course.

You will find here:

  • the lecture notes, as well as their sources (LaTeX, Inkscape and SageMath files)
  • the slides and videos of the lectures
  • some SageMath notebooks for computations involved in the course

Éric Gourgoulhon / 2022-01-15
